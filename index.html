How it works

Make your upcoming vacation pleasurable in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us the types of places you like

Tell us the types of locations you like like sea sides, mountains, historical sites. My India AI will search for all the perfect trips just for you. Our organisation will make every vacation memorable and exciting, no matter what places you like in India.

02

Approve your vacation plan

Choose the traval plan you like and approve it. You can also remove the non-compulsury traval locations from the plan. If none of the plans are of your liking then contact us at our email address with your own plans.

03

Choose your economic plan

Our organisation provide 3 types of economic plans : economic, smart and luxury. The economic plan is budget-friendly, luxury plan is the most expensive and smart combines the pros of both economic and luxury plans.

04

Easy canceling

If for any reason you want to cancel a trip a email would be enough. But it should be done atleast a week before the trip. To cancel in the week before the trip some fine has to be paid.
